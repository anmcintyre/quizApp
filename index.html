The inventor of the chocolate chip cookie, Ruth Wakefield, sold her cookie recipe to Nestle in exchange for what?

Correct

Ruth Wakefield sold her cookie recipe to Nestle for a lifetime supply of chocolate.
When were the first Theobroma Cacao beans fermented, roasted, and ground to create chocolate?

Correct

The first use of chocolate can be traced to the Mokaya and other pre-Olmec people who lived in southeast Mexico around 1900 B.C.
Which ancient culture used chocolate as currency?

Correct

The Mayans and the Aztecs used cocao beans as currency. Some would create counterfeit beans by carving them out of clay and painting them.
Mars named the Snickers bar after what?

Correct

Snickers was the name of the favorite horse of the Mars family.
Research suggests which of the following is true about chocolate?

Correct

Research suggests that dark chocolate boosts memory, attention span, reaction time, and problem-solving skills by increasing blood flow to the brain. Studies have also found that dark chocolate can improve the ability to see in low-contrast situations (such as poor weather) and promote lower blood pressure, which has positive effects on cholesterol levels, platelet function, and insulin sensitivity.
